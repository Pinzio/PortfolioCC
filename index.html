About Me - Carola Caccamo

I am a passionate UI/UX Designer driven by creativity and problem-solving, dedicated to improving user experiences and simplifying life through design. My skills include accessibility, user research, prototyping, wireframing, and graphic design, with a strong understanding of design principles. I’ve worked in diverse fields, from lead generation in digital commerce to UX design for websites, and my background in photography has sharpened my visual aesthetics. Fluent in Italian and proficient in English, I am always eager to collaborate and deliver impactful design solutions.
